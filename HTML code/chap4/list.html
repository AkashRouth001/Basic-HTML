step to make tea

  1. Heat water: Fill a kettle with water and bring it to a boil.
  2. Prepare your tea: Place your chosen tea bag or loose leaf tea in your mug or teapot.
  3. Pour water over tea: Pour the boiling water over the tea.
  4. Steep: Let the tea steep for the recommended time (usually a few minutes) depending on the tea type.
  5. Add milk and sugar (optional): If desired, add milk and sugar to taste
  6. Strain (if using loose leaf tea): Strain the tea into your cup to remove loose leaves.
